Operational Protocols - System Reference
01 AI Deterrence Sequence
AI deterrence is active in ARM AWAY mode only. After the 10-second entry delay expires, the AI voice agent activates and issues verbal warnings to the intruder through the external speaker. The agent engages up to 4 times - after which the 12V siren relay fires. In ARM HOME and ARM NIGHT modes the AI is bypassed entirely: the speakers are inside the property, engaging the AI would reveal family presence and waste critical seconds. In those modes the siren fires directly after the entry delay.
02 Entry Delay Window
Entry delay duration depends on the active arm mode:

ARM HOME - 5-second delay on door or vibration trigger. You are awake and can react quickly - open the dashboard and press DISARM before the countdown ends.

ARM AWAY - 10-second delay on all sensor types. Designed for arriving home through the front door.

ARM NIGHT - No delay. Any door or vibration breach fires the siren instantly. If you need to move through the home at night, disarm first.
03 Siren Engagement
The 12V siren engages differently depending on mode:

ARM HOME / ARM NIGHT - Siren fires immediately after the entry delay (5s or 0s respectively). No AI phase.

ARM AWAY - Siren fires after the AI deterrence phase is exhausted (4 verbal exchanges). Once active, the relay drives continuously until disarmed from the dashboard. The relay is wired active-LOW: the signal is held HIGH at rest so the siren stays off even if the ESP32-S3 restarts unexpectedly. Siren events are logged automatically in the Event Log under SIREN_ACTIVE.
04 Remote Disarm Procedure
Open the dashboard on any device and press DISARM. The command is sent to the backend over HTTPS, forwarded to the ESP32-S3 on the next heartbeat poll (within ~2 seconds), and the system transitions to DISARMED. The relay is de-energised, the AI agent loop exits, and any active siren stops. The status banner on your dashboard updates in real-time via WebSocket. No app installation required - the dashboard runs in any modern browser.
05 Sensor Node Registration
Sensor nodes (ESP32-C3) register automatically the first time they transmit over ESP-NOW to the central unit (ESP32-S3). The central unit forwards the node identity to the backend, which creates a sensor record with status ONLINE. Nodes appear in the Sensors page immediately after first contact. If a node goes offline (no heartbeat for 24 hours), its status transitions to OFFLINE automatically. The central gateway unit has a shorter 90-second timeout. Re-powering the node triggers re-registration with no manual action required.
06 Arm Modes Explained
ARM AWAY - Full perimeter active. All sensor types (motion, door, vibration) trigger the alarm with a 10-second entry delay, followed by AI voice deterrence, then the siren. Use when the premises are unoccupied.

ARM HOME - Perimeter protection with interior motion suppressed. Door and window vibration sensors trigger a 5-second entry delay, then the siren fires directly - no AI phase. Motion sensors are silenced so family members can move freely. Use when occupants are awake inside.

ARM NIGHT - Maximum immediate protection. Door and window vibration sensors fire the siren instantly with zero delay. Motion sensors are silenced so sleeping occupants can move freely. Use when everyone is in bed.
