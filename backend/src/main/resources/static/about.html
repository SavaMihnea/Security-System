Project Dossier - Classification: Open
Beyond Surveillance.
Active AI Intercept.

In an era where traditional security systems rely on passive noise and delayed response, VOXWALL redefines the boundary of residential protection. Conventional alarms have become a predictable nuisance rather than a true deterrent, failing to discourage determined intruders or provide meaningful, real-time intervention during a breach.

VOXWALL was engineered as an autonomous security layer, deploying a sophisticated AI Voice Agent to confront threats the moment a perimeter is compromised. Powered by a dedicated ESP32-S3 central processor and an ultra-low latency neural backend, the system transitions from verbal negotiation to tactical high-decibel sirens with precise, calculated escalation.

The mission is to provide professional-grade, AI-driven home defense that functions with surgical reliability. VOXWALL bridges the critical gap between simple surveillance and active prevention, ensuring that a property is not just monitored, but actively defended through intelligent, real-time deterrent protocols.

Operation Timeline
01
January 2026
Threat Assessment & Research
Analysis of residential burglary trends across Romania. Market research confirming the gap between passive alarms and active deterrence. Decision to build rather than buy.
02
February – March 2026
Hardware Architecture
Selection of ESP32-S3 as the central intelligence unit. Design of the ESP-NOW sensor mesh using ESP32-C3 nodes. First prototype of door, vibration, and motion sensors. Integration of MAX98357A audio amplifier and INMP441 microphone for full-duplex AI conversation capability.
03
March – April 2026
AI Core Development
Integration of OpenAI GPT-4 for real-time intruder conversation. ElevenLabs TTS for natural voice output. Implementation of the staged alarm protocol: 10-second entry delay, 3-dialogue AI deterrence phase, then 12V siren relay activation. Spring Boot backend deployed on Railway with MySQL persistence and WebSocket live dashboard.
04
June 2026
Field Testing & Iteration
Real-world deployment in a residential setting in Cluj-Napoca. Debugging of ESP-NOW packet loss, relay active-LOW behaviour, INMP441 DC offset filtering, and AI loop exit conditions. Iterative refinement of the voice deterrence persona and entry delay timing.
05
July 2026
Production Launch
Live deployment at voxwall-security.com. Full sensor node mesh operational. Remote arm/disarm via web dashboard. AI deterrence system active and field-proven. VOXWALL transitions from student project to production security infrastructure.
